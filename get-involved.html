How your help helps us

We believe in transparency. And in cultivating a community based around accountability and transparency, we want you to know where your donations go towards.

Community Outreach

We’re all about giving back. A portion of your total donations is dedicated to creating volunteer opportunities, operating food drive events, and raising non-profit awareness for lesser known charities with a passion for change.

Improving the App

Our mobile-giving app is in it’s infancy. We have huge plans for the future of The Giving App and your support will help us to create the best charitable-giving experience. Ever.

Supporting Charities

Simply put, a portion of your donations will go directly towards charities on our platform. In leading with transparency, it is fundamental that you know where and how much of your donations is transferred to said organizations.

Other ways to contribute

Donate

To show your support, you can donate to The Giving App directly. All donations will be used to support our platform, fund charity events, and create opportunities for our community.
