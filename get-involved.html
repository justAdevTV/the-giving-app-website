How Your Donations Help Us

Community Outreach

Along with helping the world, we also care about our own backyard. So, part of your donations will be allocated to our annual Community Outreach Budget. This fund is dedicated to organizing volunteer opportunities, Give Back days, and large-scale Giving Truck events where we serve our local communities in need.

Improving the App

Knowing that our greatest global impact will take place directly through the app, weâ€™ve allocated a portion of our budget to its operation and development. We hope to provide the best giving experience to donors and charities alike, and your donations will help make to this possible.

Supporting Charities

A big part of our mission is to support trustworthy not-for-profit organizations. So naturally, we have a budget dedicated to finding them. With this fund, we pay for premium services related to researching, vetting, and onboarding new charities. Once they're approved, you can support them through the app!
