Welcome to The Giving App, A good social network.

We're Changing the Game

Here at The Giving App, we want to bring your inner philanthropist to life. If you've ever wanted to give back by donating your time or money to a worthy cause, then you know how difficult it can be to find reputable charities that you connect with. That’s why we’ve brought the world of charitable giving to your fingertips. We believe that people are good- now let’s show the world that people care.

We Encourage Transparency

How do you know if your time or money are being put to good use? We’ve taken care of that for you. All organizations are verified through our vetting process built on accountability, trustworthiness, and measurable impact. Along with this, we encourage every organization to be transparent with our community so that you’ll be able to communicate with them directly and stay up to date with their good work!

How It Works

Giving, simplified

Getting a grasp on the philanthropic landscape can be a little tough. We've taken care of the hassle for you!
- Easily find causes you care about
- Follow the journeys of the organizations you support
- Inspire others by sharing donations with the world

We Are a Movement

We’re more than just a mobile app, we are a movement - a community with members all across the country and, soon, the world. By partnering with an array of 501(c)(3) organizations (food banks, shelters, hospitals, youth outreach programs, wildlife rescues, etc.) we are taking over the charitable giving space. Our team is led by a diverse group of entrepreneurs, engineers, visionaries, tech junkies, and business developers who have spent years crafting this idea and nurturing it into a global force for good.
