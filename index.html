We are taking a stand

In essence, “there is no power for change greater than a community discovering what it cares about”. We are a community who cares about people, giving graciously of what is ours, and believing that WE will change the world… together.

Giving, simplified

We are a non-profit organization dedicated to doing extraordinary things with ordinary people. We are a community who believes that a better world is possible for millions, and through our mobile app, we will create a platform that makes giving back easy, fun, and transparent.
