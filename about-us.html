James MizukiFOUNDER & CEO

Our Values

1

We are grateful

We are all so blessed, and it’s our deep gratitude for life that humbles and drives us to work hard, give back, and positively impact the world around us.

2

We are a team

We show up for one another, we seek to grow together, and we lead with the mindset of genuine care for one other.

3

We are learners

We’re only human after all! So we are leaders who cultivate a safe space for our team to learn and improve as we go. “We’ll Figure it out.”

4

We are inclusive

We appreciate our differences and find joy in the fact that we don’t need to share the same views, identities, or beliefs in order to love one another and to support one another toward our greater common goal.

5

We have belief

We are a Purpose-Driven Team that perseveres with optimism and passion because we believe in one another and because we believe that our mission is important.

6

We have integrity

We value honesty, accountability, and the content of our character.

7

We have fun

Because why not?! We value a culture of friendship and happiness which increases our opportunity to naturally and positively impact the world.
